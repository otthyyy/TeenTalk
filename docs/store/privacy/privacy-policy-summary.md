# Privacy Policy Summary for Store Reviewers

This document provides a high-level summary of TeenTalk's privacy practices for App Store and Google Play reviewers. For the full privacy policy, visit: https://teentalk.app/privacy

---

## Overview

TeenTalk is a social platform for teens built with privacy and safety as core principles. We collect only the minimum data necessary to operate the app and provide value to users. We do not sell data, display ads, or track users across third-party apps or websites.

---

## What Data We Collect

### Account Information
- **Email address** (required for account creation)
- **Phone number** (optional, for phone authentication)
- **First name** (optional, for profile display)
- **Date of birth** (for age verification and COPPA compliance)

### User-Generated Content
- **Posts, comments, and messages** created by the user
- **Profile information** (school, bio, interests)
- **Profile photo** (optional)

### Technical Data
- **User ID** (Firebase UID for account identification)
- **Device information** (OS version, device model, for crash diagnostics)
- **Crash logs** (stack traces, error messages)
- **App usage data** (features used, session duration – aggregated and anonymized)

---

## How We Use Data

| Purpose | Data Used |
|---------|-----------|
| **Account Management** | Email, phone, user ID |
| **App Functionality** | User-generated content, profile info |
| **Safety & Moderation** | Reports, user ID, content flagged |
| **Analytics & Improvement** | Crash logs, usage data (anonymized) |
| **Support & Communication** | Email address |

---

## What We Don't Do

❌ **Sell data** to advertisers or third parties
❌ **Track users** across other apps or websites
❌ **Display ads**
❌ **Use data for targeted advertising**
❌ **Collect location data** (GPS)
❌ **Access contacts, SMS, or call logs**

---

## Data Sharing

TeenTalk does not share user data with third parties except:

1. **Service Providers** (Firebase by Google)
   - Purpose: Backend infrastructure, authentication, database, crash reporting
   - Agreement: Data Processing Agreement in place
   - Privacy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

2. **Legal Obligations**
   - In response to valid legal requests (subpoenas, court orders)
   - To protect user safety or prevent illegal activity

3. **No Advertising Networks**
   - TeenTalk does not integrate advertising SDKs

---

## User Privacy Controls

Users have full control over their data:

| Control | How to Access |
|---------|--------------|
| **Edit Profile** | Profile > Edit |
| **View Privacy Settings** | Profile > Privacy Settings |
| **Opt Out of Crash Reporting** | Profile > Privacy Settings > Crash Reporting |
| **Delete Account** | Profile > Settings > Delete Account |
| **Request Data Export** | Email support@teentalk.app |

---

## Parental Consent (COPPA Compliance)

TeenTalk is designed for teens aged 13–18. For users under 18:

- **Parental consent is required** during onboarding
- Parents can request account deletion by emailing support@teentalk.app
- We collect only data essential for app functionality
- No third-party advertising or behavioral tracking

---

## Data Retention

- **Active Accounts:** Data retained as long as the account exists
- **Deleted Accounts:** User data purged within 30 days of account deletion
- **Crash Data:** Retained for 90 days (Firebase Crashlytics policy)
- **Anonymized Analytics:** Retained indefinitely for improvement purposes

---

## Data Security

- **Encryption in Transit:** All data transmitted via HTTPS/TLS
- **Encryption at Rest:** Firebase Firestore encrypts stored data
- **Access Control:** Role-based permissions for staff
- **Firebase Security Rules:** Restrict data access to authenticated users

---

## Compliance

TeenTalk complies with:

- **COPPA** (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act)
- **GDPR** (General Data Protection Regulation)
- **CCPA** (California Consumer Privacy Act)

Users have rights to:
- Access their data
- Request data deletion
- Opt out of optional data collection (crash reporting, analytics)
- Withdraw consent

---

## Privacy by Design

TeenTalk follows privacy by design principles:

1. **Data Minimization:** Collect only what's necessary
2. **Purpose Limitation:** Use data only for stated purposes
3. **User Control:** Provide clear privacy settings
4. **Transparency:** Disclose data practices clearly
5. **Security:** Protect data with industry-standard measures

   - Open Profile > Settings
   - Tap "Privacy Policy"

4. **Parental Consent (New User Flow)**
   - Create a new account (use test email)
   - Enter date of birth for under 18 user
   - Verify parental consent prompt appears
