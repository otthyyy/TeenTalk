# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** January 2024

## Introduction

Welcome to TeenTalk ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a safe and secure experience on our platform. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information

When you create an account and use TeenTalk, we may collect:

- **Account Information:** Nickname, email address (for account recovery), age confirmation, and authentication data
- **Profile Information:** Gender (optional), school (optional), school year, interests, clubs, and profile preferences
- **User-Generated Content:** Posts, comments, messages, and other content you create on the platform
- **Usage Data:** Information about how you interact with the app, including features used, time spent, and engagement patterns

### Automatically Collected Information

- **Device Information:** Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
- **Log Data:** IP address, access times, app crashes, and performance data
- **Analytics Data:** Anonymized usage statistics to improve our services

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

- **Service Delivery:** Providing, maintaining, and improving the TeenTalk platform
- **Safety & Moderation:** Ensuring platform safety, preventing abuse, and moderating content according to our Community Guidelines
- **Communication:** Sending you notifications, updates, and responding to your requests
- **Analytics:** Understanding usage patterns and improving user experience
- **Legal Compliance:** Complying with legal obligations and protecting rights

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information:

- **With Your Consent:** When you explicitly authorize us to share information
- **For Safety:** With law enforcement or authorities when required by law or to prevent harm
- **Service Providers:** With trusted third-party services that help us operate the platform (e.g., Firebase, cloud storage)
- **Aggregated Data:** Anonymous, aggregated statistics that cannot identify individual users

## Your Rights Under GDPR

If you are in the European Economic Area, you have the following rights:

- **Right of Access:** Request a copy of your personal data
- **Right to Rectification:** Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
- **Right to Erasure:** Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten")
- **Right to Restrict Processing:** Limit how we process your data
- **Right to Data Portability:** Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
- **Right to Object:** Object to certain types of data processing
- **Right to Withdraw Consent:** Withdraw consent for data processing at any time

To exercise these rights, please contact us at privacy@teentalk.app.

## Children's Privacy

TeenTalk is designed for teenagers. We require parental consent for users under 16 years of age in the EU and under 13 in other jurisdictions. Parents or guardians can:

- Review their child's information
- Request deletion of their child's data
- Refuse further collection of their child's information

## Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

- Encryption of data in transit and at rest
- Regular security audits
- Access controls and authentication
- Secure cloud infrastructure (Firebase)

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

## Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as:

- Your account is active
- Necessary to provide our services
- Required by law or for legitimate business purposes

When you delete your account, we will delete or anonymize your personal data, except where retention is required by law.

## Anonymous Posting

TeenTalk allows anonymous posting. While anonymous posts do not display your nickname:

- We maintain internal records linking anonymous posts to user accounts for moderation purposes
- Anonymous posts are subject to the same content moderation policies
- Legal authorities may request identification of anonymous users when required by law

## Third-Party Services

We use Firebase (Google) for:

- Authentication
- Cloud storage
- Analytics
- Crash reporting

These services have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them.

## International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your residence, including the United States. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or by email. Your continued use of TeenTalk after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
